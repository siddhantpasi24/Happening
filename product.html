About

General

GCC Hotel and Club is amidst the 3-star hotels in the city of Mumbai which provides best-in-class services to its clients. From 1933, since its establishment, it has been amazing the residents of Thane, with its classy service and luxurious interiors. It is the foremost hotel to get the 3-star rating from the Ministry of Tourism. From the beginning itself, they have gained immense goodwill from its clients because of its commitment to service, hospitality and customer satisfaction. Conveniently located just 2 km from the city limits, they offer a peaceful and serene environment for your wedding and related events.

Facilities and Capacity

GCC Hotel and Club is a spectacular place for all kinds of events. Whether it is a big fat Indian wedding or a small cocktail party, its modern facilities and the sophisticated interiors will definitely impress you. GCC Hotel and Club offers more than 90,000 sq. ft. lawn space to take your wedding party to the next level. Its three spacious banquet halls can accommodate 50 to 500 people any time. With more than one event space, they also provide you with guest accommodation and basic facilities like valet parking.

Service Offered


Besides banquet halls and lush green lawns, GCC Hotel and Club offers other services like a great place to have a residential wedding with all your family enjoying the whole wedding process. It also offers different wedding vendors for you to choose from, like decorators, music, and entertainment, photography, and videography, transportation, and ticketing, mehndi and makeup. It is an ideal venue for all of your wedding-related functions.

Cusine

And to make the experience complete GCC Hotel and Club offers a great catering service with three menus to choose from, Catholic, vegetarian, and non-vegetarian, while blending all your tastes and preferences, to create a personalized spread. The cuisines which they offer include:
  • North Indian

  • South Indian

  • Chinese

  • Continental

  • North-Eastern

  • North-Western

  • Mughlai

  • Regional specific menu
